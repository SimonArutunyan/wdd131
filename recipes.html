Quinoa Salad

This salad is rich in protein and fiber, making it perfect for a light lunch.

Ingredients:

  • Quinoa - 1 cup
  • Tomatoes - 2 pcs
  • Cucumber - 1 pc
  • Olive oil - 2 tbsp
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Preparation:

Cook the quinoa, chop the vegetables, and mix all the ingredients. Drizzle with olive oil, salt, and pepper.

Berry Smoothie

This smoothie is a great way to start your morning with a dose of vitamins.

Ingredients:

  • Berries (raspberries, blueberries) - 1 cup
  • Banana - 1 pc
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Honey - 1 tbsp

Preparation:

Blend all the ingredients until smooth. Serve chilled.
